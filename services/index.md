I'm available for a limited number of external engagements each year.
If any of the following sounds like what your team needs, please
[get in touch](mailto:{{ site.email }}).

## Faculty development program design

From one-off workshops to year-long fellowships. I help teaching centers,
departments, and institutions design faculty development that actually
builds practice — with realistic time commitments, meaningful peer
structures, and evidence of impact you can point to.

## Generative AI integration

Strategy and hands-on support for institutions figuring out how to engage
with generative AI in teaching, research, and operations without either
hype or panic. Includes policy review, workshop series, and
fellowship-style programs modeled on GAIT.

## Learning analytics & data pipelines

Python, R, Power BI, Tableau. I design pipelines that turn messy
institutional data (consultations, attendance, OER usage, grant outcomes)
into dashboards and reports that answer the questions leadership is
actually asking.

## Facilitation

Retreats, community-of-practice launches, Appreciative Inquiry–based
convenings. Warm, structured, reflective — not performative.
